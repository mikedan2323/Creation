Day 7: The Sabbath Rest

God concludes His work, blessing and sanctifying the seventh day.

Understanding Day 7

Having completed the heavens, the earth, and all their hosts, God ceased His creative activity on the seventh day. He didn't rest from fatigue, but to establish a pattern and memorial. He blessed the seventh day and made it holy, setting it apart as a time for rest, worship, and remembrance of His mighty works.

  • God ceased His creative work, marking completion.
  • He blessed the seventh day, infusing it with divine favor.
  • He sanctified it, setting it apart as holy time.
  • The Sabbath is a memorial of Creation, instituted at Creation week's end.
  • Links directly to the 4th Commandment (Exodus 20:8-11).
