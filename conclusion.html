Creation's Enduring Message

From the beginning of time to the end of earth's history, God's creative power remains the foundation of our faith and worship. The seven-day creation account is not merely ancient history, but a living testimony to God's character and His ongoing relationship with humanity.

Why Creation Matters Today:

  • It reveals God as our personal, powerful Creator who formed us in His image.
  • It establishes the Sabbath as a perpetual memorial of God's creative work.
  • It provides the foundation for understanding redemption - the same God who created can re-create.
  • It calls us to worship the Creator in this final era of earth's history (Rev. 14:7).
  • It reminds us of our role as stewards of God's creation.

A Living Memorial

The Sabbath stands as a weekly reminder of creation week, linking us back to Eden and forward to the earth made new. Each Sabbath is an invitation to rest in God's completed work and celebrate His creative power.
