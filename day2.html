Day 2: The Firmament

God separates the waters above from the waters below.

Understanding Day 2

Continuing His work of ordering the cosmos, God spoke the "firmament" or "expanse" into existence. This act separated the waters, creating the atmosphere and defining the realms above and below.

  • God continues bringing order, creating the atmosphere.
  • Separates waters above (atmospheric moisture/clouds) from waters below (seas/oceans).
  • This prepared the way for weather patterns and habitable land.
  • God names the expanse "Heaven" (or Sky).
  • "Evening and morning" confirms the second 24-hour day.
