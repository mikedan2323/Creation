Day 5: Sea Creatures & Birds

God fills the waters and skies with abundant, moving life.

Understanding Day 5

The focus shifts to creatures with "nephesh" (soul, life force, consciousness). God commanded the waters to teem with living creatures and the skies to be filled with birds. He created great sea creatures and every winged bird, blessing them to be fruitful and multiply.

  • Introduction of conscious animal life ('nephesh').
  • Waters filled with swarming creatures.
  • Skies filled with birds of every kind.
  • First explicit blessing given: "Be fruitful and multiply."

Genesis 1:20-23 (NKJV)

20Then God said, "Let the waters abound with an abundance of living creatures, and let birds fly above the earth across the face of the firmament of the heavens." 21So God created great sea creatures and every living thing that moves, with which the waters abounded, according to their kind, and every winged bird according to its kind. And God saw that it was good. 22And God blessed them, saying, "Be fruitful and multiply, and fill the waters in the seas, and let birds multiply on the earth." 23So the evening and the morning were the fifth day.
